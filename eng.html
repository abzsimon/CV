About

I did my M.A in Philosophy and Litterature in Paris and Berlin.

After my studies I worked as an administrative assistant at the French Institute for Demographic Studies, and took part to the organisation of a queer feminist festival. I started dancing as well (classical, contemporary, contact improvisation).

I then decided to turn my hobby into a job and worked for almost two years a cook in a vegetarian restaurant located in the theater of the International University Campus.

I actually work as a grant manager at the French National Center for Scientific Research, helping more than 50 research units applying for international, national and european funding.

In the meanwhile I also study web development and I translate philosophical texts from german to french.
